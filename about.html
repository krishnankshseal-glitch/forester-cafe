how we got here

About Greenshack

A greenhouse that grew a coffee bar, instead of the other way around.

the beginning

A greenhouse with too many cuttings.

Greenshack started in 2016 in a backyard greenhouse that had quietly taken over a family's weekends. There were more propagated cuttings than pots to put them in, and friends kept staying long after they'd come to collect one — mostly for the coffee someone had started brewing on a camp stove between the shelves. Within a year the greenhouse had a proper counter, and within two it had a name.

what we believe

Plants first. Everything else follows.

We think a room full of living plants changes how food and coffee taste in it — a little slower, a little calmer. So the greenery isn't styling. The herbs on your toast were probably alive an hour ago. The hanging pothos over the counter needs repotting as often as the espresso machine needs descaling, and both get done on schedule.

We buy produce from three growers we know by name, compost everything that can be composted, and haven't ordered a single-use cup since 2019.
