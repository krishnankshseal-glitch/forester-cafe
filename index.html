our story

It started in a greenhouse, not a kitchen.

Greenshack began as a family greenhouse with too many cuttings and not enough customers. Now it's a cafe where the coffee bar sits in the middle of the plants that inspire it — herbs steeped fresh, produce sourced from growers we know by name, and a swap shelf where regulars trade cuttings for house credit.
